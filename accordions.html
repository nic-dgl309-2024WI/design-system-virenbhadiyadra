11 Accordions

The acc Section in our Immortal Design System is a classy and adaptable feature. It provides an elegant method of arranging and presenting content, drawing inspiration from the musical instrument. Information can be easily expanded and collapsed by users, resulting in a dynamic interface yet easy to use.
