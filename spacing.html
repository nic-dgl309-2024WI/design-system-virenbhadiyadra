03 Spacing

Margin and Padding:-

  • mr - a class to represent margin.

  • pd - a class to represent padding.

Different sides:-

  • t - a class to assign margin-top or padding-top.

  • r - a class to assign margin-right or padding-right.

  • b - a class to assign margin-bottom or padding-bottom.

  • l - a class to assign margin-left or padding-left.

  • x - a class to assign margin or padding to left and right.

  • y - a class to assign margin or padding to top and bottom.

  • all - a class to assign margin or padding to all sides.

Different sizes:-

  • 0 - a class to reset the margin or padding by 0.

  • 1 - a class to assign the margin or padding to .5rem.

  • 2 - a class to assign the margin or padding to .75rem.

  • 3 - a class to assign the margin or padding to 1rem.

  • 4 - a class to assign the margin or padding to 1.5rem.

  • 5 - a class to assign the margin or padding to 3.50rem.

  • auto - a class to assign the margin or padding to auto.
