05 Containers

Containers will help you align your content within a specified width.Enter the Container Section, which is at the core of our design philosophy. In our digital world, these adaptable containers serve as the foundation for both structural and visual harmony. Welcome to the hub of our design journey, whether you're a developer figuring out our system or a designer looking for consistency.

Implementation

Containers are basic layout components in Immortal Design; they are necessary for our default grid system. They function to keep the content contained, padding it, and occasionally to center it. Although Immortal Design containers allow for nesting, most layouts do not require this extra layer of hierarchy.

The table below will show you how each container size works.
